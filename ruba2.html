2.Antipodean Café, KL


Antipodean Café is sure to please anyone who loves a long, leisurely breakfast, whether you are a fan of a hearty burrito wrap or healthy avocado on toast. Quaint yet hip, this compact café got a lot of locals talking about its all-day breakfast menu when it first emerged on the city’s foodie scene. Before it came about, breakfast bars were not particularly rampant in the city – largely because Malaysians patronised kopitiams (coffee shops), which serve local food, more for their morning meal. With the emergence of Antipodean Café, the city’s food scene changed dramatically, with more and more young, trendy urbanites frequenting them.

Recommended menu :

hearty burrito wrap and healthy avocado on toast.

Sun – Thu 08:00 – 22:00, Fri – Sat 08:00 – 23:00
